The Tunday kababs at Tunday Kababi serves the authentic kebabs. Tunday Kababi represents the culinary delights that Lucknow is so famous for. The royal taste of this place comes from the Nawabs cuisine.
Loction : 168/6, Old Nazirabad Rd, Mohan Market, Khayali Ganj, Aminabad, Lucknow

Biryani at Idrees ki Biryaniis a must visit place for its juicy mutton biryani. This 45-year-old shop in Chowk is being run by Abu Bakr, the third generation of his family to carry on the legacy of serving mouth-watering biryani.
Loction : Patanala Chauraha, Opposite Patanala police station, Chowk

Basket Chat at Royale Cafe flavours that will burst in your mouth in a single bite.The basket is deep-fried and then filled with matar, tikki chutneys with a garnishing of pomegranate, sev and aromatic spices.
Location:Royale Cafe, HazratGanj, Lucknow

Kulfi at Prakash Kulfi.The oldest market of Lucknow has the most famous kulfi wala. You cannot go away without trying their kulfi which is best enjoyed with falooda. .Prakash kulfi is serving faluda kulfi in Lucknow since ages and nothing has beaten them
Location: 12, 13, 1st Ln, Mohan Market, Khayali Ganj, Aminabad, Lucknow

Rahim ki Kulcha Nahari. tender pieces of meat marinated in an exotic spice rub and cooked overnight, stuffed into freshly baked tandoori kulche. After tunday kebabs, nahari kulcha is the second most famous food jewel of Lucknow food and cuisine. All you need to do is imagine heaven on a plate.
Location: Akbari Gate, Chowk, Lucknow

Sheermal at Chowk Sheermal or Shirmal is a very famous delicacy prepared in Lucknow.It is a saffron-coloured naan or flatbread.It is enjoyed with a cup of chai or even on its own. You must try this dish on your visit to Lucknow. The dish is also prepared in Hyderabad but no ones does it better than the Lucknowis.
Location: chowk, Lucknow
