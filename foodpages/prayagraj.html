The Chole samosa at Sainik Sweets, Ashok Nagar must not be missed by anyone visiting Allahabad. The potato-filled samosa is crushed and served with spicy chhole along with curd, tamarind chutney, onion, coriander and sev to make this street food of Allahabad a hit.
Loction : Sainik Sweets ,Muir Rd, Rajapur, Allahabad

Lassi at Rajaram Lassi waala is a must visit place for its various sweet and fragrant flavourings. This place is serving its aromatic and soothing flavours from past 120 years
Loction : Rajaram Lassi waala, 18/36, Loknath Lane, Chowk, Malviyanagar, Allahabad

Chicken Biryani at Eaton is the centre of attraction to all the biryani lovers here. And if you think that biryani is expensive, the price here will make you ask for more servings
Location:Eat On, 16, south nawab yusuf road ,Allahabad

Kachori Sabzi at Netram and Mulchand Sons. If you think that Delhi serves the best kachori and sabzi as a breakfast option, then you need to visit this place in Allahabad and the crowd will give you the answer. Here, the kachoris are deep fried in desi ghee and are filled with urad dal that has a spicy and tangy after taste.
Location: 259, Katra Chouraha, Katra, Allahabad

Khasta damaloo at Loknath. Normally khasta is served with aloo tamatar ki sabzi or with khatti metti hari laal chutney. But, in Allahabad, khasta is also served with Dum Aloo. If you love having crispy khasta then you must try this combination at least once.
Location: 322/12,Loknath lane, Allahabad

Gulab Jamun at Dehati Rasgulla. The place's name says rasgulla but they serve Gulab jamun. this place is famous for it super delicious and soft gulaab jamun made with pure khoya and fresh milk.
Location: 389/329, MG Marg Madhwapur Sabji Mandi, Bairhana, Prayagraj
